ABOUT ME

what I love to do

As an enthusiastic technology educator, I am fascinated by delivering high-quality computer science education to students, and help them reach their full potential. I believe that building digital literacy is an essential way to prepare our next generation for a tech-driven future. Let's do it together!

Experience in technology education

With a background in both Electronic Engineering and Education, I acquired Microsoft Innovative Educator Certification. 6 years of experience in teaching programming in school classrooms and community educational institutions, 8 years of experience in software development in a large IT company. With experience in coaching students for Computing/STEM contests, I am always looking to introduce Computer Science classes in an effective and creative way!
